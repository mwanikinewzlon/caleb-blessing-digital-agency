About Us

Kingdom Digital Store &
Caleb Blessing Agency

Founded on September 15, 2025, Caleb Blessing Digital Agency & Consultancy was built with one mission: to empower businesses and individuals through transformative digital solutions.

Kingdom Digital Store is our flagship marketplace — a platform combining classified listings (like Jiji) with a full marketplace system (like Jumia) but powered by WhatsApp-first communication for instant buyer-seller connection.

Together, we have served 15+ international companies worldwide and helped thousands of Kenyans sell, buy, and grow online.
